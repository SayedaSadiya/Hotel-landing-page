Room Types

Single Occupancy Room

A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds. The room size or area of Single Room is usually between 37msq to 130 msq.

Family Room

A parlour or living room connected with one or more bedrooms.(A room with one or more bedrooms and a separate living area). The room size or area of Family Room is usually between 70msq to 100 msq.

Duplex Room

The most expensive room in the hotel, with a separate living area and bedroom on two floors. Similar to a normal suite, a Duplex suite always has one or more bedrooms, a living space with a strong emphasis on comfort, high-quality amenities and supplies, and tailored services(e.g. personal butler during the stay).
